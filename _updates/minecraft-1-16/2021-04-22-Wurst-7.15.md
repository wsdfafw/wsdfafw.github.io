This version of the Wurst Hacked Client is available for Minecraft 1.16.5, 1.16.4 and 1.16.3, as well as snapshots 21w19a, 21w18a, 21w17a, 21w16a and 21w15a.

## Changelog

- Added [AnchorAura](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/anchoraura): Automatically places (optional), charges, and detonates respawn anchors to kill entities around you.

- Added a ["Sneak at edges" checkbox](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/safewalk#sneak_at_edges) to SafeWalk.

- [BonemealAura](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/bonemealaura) will now prioritize the furthest blocks instead of the closest.

- Searching for "lag switch" in Navigator will now find [Blink](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/blink).

- Fixed a crash when using Wurst together with Tweakeroo.

- Fixed a rare crash when using [AutoFarm](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/autofarm).

- Added deepslate copper ore and deepslate coal ore to [X-Ray](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/xray)'s default list of ores. (Minecraft 21w18a+)

- Fixed the spammy errors in the log files when using Minecraft 21w18a and later versions.

- Fixed [MobESP](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/mobesp)'s tracer lines not rendering correctly in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.

- Fixed [Search](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/search) crashing the game in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.

- Fixed BlockSettings (e.g. [Search Block](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/search#block)) not rendering correctly in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.

### GUIs:

- Window-based [ClickGUI](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/clickgui) (press **Right CTRL** to open).

- [Navigator](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/navigator) (press **Right Shift** to open).

- TabGUI (必须通过导航器启用, use with **Arrow Keys** and **Enter**).

### Keybinds

**All keybinds can be changed in-game.** Go to Wurst Options > Keybinds or type [`.help binds`](/tutorials/dot-binds-command/) in the chat.

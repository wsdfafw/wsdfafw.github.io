This version of the Wurst Hacked Client is available for Minecraft 1.16.5, 1.16.4 and 1.16.3, as well as snapshots 21w19a, 21w18a, 21w17a, 21w16a and 21w15a.

## Changelog

- Added [AnchorAura](https://wurst.wiki/anchoraura): Automatically places (optional), charges, and detonates respawn anchors to kill entities around you.

- Added a ["Sneak at edges" checkbox](https://wurst.wiki/safewalk#sneak_at_edges) to SafeWalk.

- [BonemealAura](https://wurst.wiki/bonemealaura) will now prioritize the furthest blocks instead of the closest.

- Searching for "lag switch" in Navigator will now find [Blink](https://wurst.wiki/blink).

- Fixed a crash when using Wurst together with Tweakeroo.

- Fixed a rare crash when using [AutoFarm](https://wurst.wiki/autofarm).

- Added deepslate copper ore and deepslate coal ore to [X-Ray](https://wurst.wiki/xray)'s default list of ores. (Minecraft 21w18a+)

- Fixed the spammy errors in the log files when using Minecraft 21w18a and later versions.

- Fixed [MobESP](https://wurst.wiki/mobesp)'s tracer lines not rendering correctly in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.

- Fixed [Search](https://wurst.wiki/search) crashing the game in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.

- Fixed BlockSettings (e.g. [Search Block](https://wurst.wiki/search#block)) not rendering correctly in Minecraft 21w19a and later versions.
